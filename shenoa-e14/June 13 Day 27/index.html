Morning Lectures

Maps in Practice

Maps in JavaScript are a collection of key-value pairs where keys can be of any type, and they preserve the order of insertion, offering efficient methods for adding, deleting, and iterating over elements.

Sets in Practice

Sets in JavaScript are a collection of unique values, allowing you to store any type of value (primitives or object references) without duplicates, and they provide efficient methods for adding, deleting, and checking the presence of elements.
