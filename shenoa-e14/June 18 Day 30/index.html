Bubble Sort in Practice

Bubble sort is a simple sorting algorithm that repeatedly steps through the list, compares adjacent elements, and swaps them if they are in the wrong order, effectively "bubbling" the largest unsorted element to its correct position in each pass.

Insertion Sort

Insertion sort is a straightforward sorting algorithm that builds the final sorted array one item at a time by repeatedly taking the next element and inserting it into its correct position among the previously sorted elements.
